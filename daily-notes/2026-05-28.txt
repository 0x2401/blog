India
- History of India
  - What even is the history of India?
    - Colonized by the British Empire via chartered merchant companies. Inhabited by primarily Muslims and Hindus. Ruled by the British for almost a century(?) Protested for independence in the aftermath of the Second World War amidst broader decolonization. Eventually, Britain relented and granted Indian independence. Significant figure in this process was Mahatma Gandhi, a lawyer and political activist who engaged in hunger strikes to pressure Britain.
    - Hindus and Muslims conflicted with each other. Eventual partition of India led to the creation of the modern Indian state, and Pakistan (which included what would eventually become Bangladesh.) The partition of India caused mass migration of peoples to either of the two new countries, causing millions of deaths.
    - Modern India proceeded to grow. Had an aerospace engineer president. Now ruled by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. Under Modi's leadership, there have been significant crackdowns on the Muslim minority and democratic backsliding under the BJP.
